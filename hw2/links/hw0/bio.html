Bio
My name is Howard (Hank) Clark. I was born in Silver Spring, Maryland and I lived in northern Virginia until I was in the fourth grade. Because of my dad's job, my family moved to Colorado where I have lived ever since. My favorite place is my Grandfather's house in Northern Maine. He lives on Moosehead Lake, and during summers as a kid my family would visit for up to a month to enjoy sailing, water skiing, and hiking while spending time with extended family. I am currently a Sophomore at Yale and am a member of Trumbull College. I plan on majoring in chemistry.
